More than a Bookstore

Visit for a book. Stay for a community.

We are known for our wide selection, local events, and handwritten book recommendations along the shelves.

In 1975, we opened the doors of Oblong Books in Millerton, NY and we have been supporting our community ever since.

We have fundraised for Red Hook Public Library, Bard College, Rhinebeck Farmers Market and more!
